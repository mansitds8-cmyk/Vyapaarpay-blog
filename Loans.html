What Are Loans?

Loans are borrowed money you repay with interest over time. Banks, NBFCs, or fintech platforms like Vyapaarpay lend cash for specific needs,secured by assets or your creditworthiness.

Think of it as a financial bridge. Need a wedding fund? Education fees? Or business expansion? Loans cover it all without draining savings.

Types of Loans in India

India's loan market is diverse. Here's a quick rundown of popular types.

Personal Loan

No collateral needed, and you can use the money for almost anything like travel, weddings, medical bills, or home renovation. It is flexible, but the interest rates tend to be higher since there is no security for the lender.

Home Loan

Planning to buy a house? A home loan (or mortgage) lets you borrow a large sum and repay it over 10–30 years. It typically comes with lower interest rates because your property acts as collateral.

Car Loan

Whether it is a liftback or an SUV, a car loan helps you drive home your dream vehicle today and pay for it in comfortable monthly installments. The car itself is the collateral here.

Education Loan

Investing in yourself is always a good idea. Education loans cover tuition fees, living expenses, and study materials. Many come with a moratorium period meaning you don't have to start repaying until after you graduate.

Business Loan

Got a startup idea or need capital to scale? Business loans are designed to help entrepreneurs fund operations, buy equipment, or manage cash flow. Terms vary widely based on your business profile.
